# GlacioAware

## Climate Intelligence & Environmental Resilience Platform

GlacioAware is an interdisciplinary environmental intelligence initiative exploring climate resilience, flood vulnerability, sustainability, and geospatial environmental systems through remote sensing, spatial analytics, and human-centered climate communication.

This platform was developed to bridge environmental science, geospatial technology, and sustainability-focused digital storytelling in climate-sensitive regions, with a particular focus on Bangladesh and South Asia.

## Research Highlights

### IFVIF — Integrated Flood Vulnerability and Impact Framework
A multidimensional flood vulnerability framework integrating:
- flood exposure,
- socioeconomic sensitivity,
- adaptive capacity,
- population vulnerability,
- and spatial environmental indicators.

### SAR Flood Inundation Mapping
Sentinel-1 SAR-based flood extent analysis for Bangladesh (2015–2024) using cloud-independent remote sensing workflows.

### NDVI Vegetation Stress Analysis
Temporal vegetation analysis using Sentinel-2 imagery to examine flood-related agricultural and ecological stress patterns.

### Spatial Vulnerability Clustering
Application of Moran’s I and Getis-Ord Gi* statistics for identifying statistically significant flood vulnerability hotspots.

## Why GlacioAware Exists

Growing up in Bangladesh — one of the world’s most climate-vulnerable countries — inspired a deep interest in understanding how environmental intelligence, sustainability-focused technology, and geospatial analysis can contribute to climate resilience and adaptive capacity.

GlacioAware was created to explore how interdisciplinary environmental research can become more accessible, human-centered, and socially relevant.
